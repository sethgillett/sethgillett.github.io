Performances

We sing in English, Hebrew, Ladino, Luganda, and Yiddish, and we'd be delighted to perform at your wedding, bar/bat mitzvah, synagogue event, or other special occasion! Our rate is $180 per 15 minutes of music performed, but we are able to negotiate depending on the circumstances.
